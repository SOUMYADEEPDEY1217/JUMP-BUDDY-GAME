# 🎮 **JUMP BUDDY**

## 📖 Description
A charming, retro-style 2D pixel art platformer. Take control of a futuristic, visor-wearing hero as you navigate through a whimsical world made of wooden blocks and pink frosting. Time your jumps perfectly to avoid patrolling pink snails, collect the red apples scattered across floating platforms, and make your way to the checkered flag to complete the level!

## ✨ Features
* **Classic Platforming Action:** Jump across floating platforms, navigate gaps, and dodge hazards.
* **Retro Pixel Art Aesthetic:** Nostalgic visuals featuring a colorful, dessert-themed environment.
* **Collectibles:** Gather shiny red apples to boost your score.
* **Enemies:** Strategize your movements to avoid the slow but steady pink snails.
* **Clear Objectives:** Reach the checkered flag to conquer the stage.
